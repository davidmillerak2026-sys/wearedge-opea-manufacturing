## Publication Integrity Rule

A row may move to `Published` only after a public URL is opened or otherwise verified. Prepared copy, a draft, an attempted submission, or an authenticated editor page does not count as publication.

The tracker deliberately does not claim publication on platforms for which this execution environment has no authenticated connector or supported browser-control channel.
